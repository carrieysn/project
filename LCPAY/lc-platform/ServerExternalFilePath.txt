银信证平台各应用系统所依赖的应用外部配置文件路径约定如下。开发环境除外，因为开发环境使用应用内置的配置文件。

======================================
Gateway应用配置文件
======================================
/app/cifpaylc/gateway/config/gateway-config.xml
/app/cifpaylc/gateway/config/logback-config.xml

======================================
【标准】银信证核心业务应用配置文件
======================================
/app/cifpaylc/standard-business/config/jdbc.properties
/app/cifpaylc/standard-business/config/system-config.xml
/app/cifpaylc/standard-business/config/logback-config.xml

======================================
【标准】银信证定时应用配置文件
======================================
/app/cifpaylc/standard-scheduler/config/jdbc.properties
/app/cifpaylc/standard-scheduler/config/system-config.xml
/app/cifpaylc/standard-scheduler/config/logback-config.xml


======================================
【非标准】银信证核心业务应用配置文件
======================================
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-business/config/jdbc.properties
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-business/config/system-config.xml
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-business/config/icbc-bankdirect.xml
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-business/config/logback-config.xml

======================================
【非标准】银信证定时应用配置文件
======================================
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-scheduler/config/jdbc.properties
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-scheduler/config/system-config.xml
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-scheduler/config/logback-config.xml
/app/cifpaylc/nonstandard-scheduler/config/icbc-bankdirect.xml
